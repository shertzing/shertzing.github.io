I am reluctant to count the days since his death. Today I did. 53. Fifty-three days since we said goodbye forever. In my confusion I read the devotional for the day after his death. God in His kindness ordained it so: Isaiah 46:4 I will be your God throughout your lifetime–until your hair is white with age.
